Chart
Pattern

Trading chart patterns are graphical representations of market trends and price movements over a specific period of time. By analyzing these patterns, traders can gain insights into market behavior, identify potential buying and selling opportunities, and make informed trading decisions.

Candle
Stick

Candlesticks are a type of chart pattern used in technical analysis of financial markets. Each candlestick displays the high, low, opening and closing prices of an asset during a specific time frame.
